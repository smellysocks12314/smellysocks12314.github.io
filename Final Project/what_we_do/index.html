Outreaches

Outreach is a core part of VJ Astro! Using our equipment and knowledge in the field, we spread the love of astronomy to various schools. Some of our partner schools include Tanjong Katong Girls School, Victoria School and CHIJ St Theresa's Convent

Overseas Trips

Travelling overseas allows VJ Astro to soak in the beauty of a light-pollution-free nightsky. We organize a bi-annual overseas trip to various different regional locations, bringing our equipment and appetite for the night sky. Past locations include Kahang and Mersing.

Outreaches

An astronomy club is nothing without stargazing! We have weekly stargazing sessions on Friday nights at East Coast Park/VJC school field. Bringing out our telescopes, those sessions are a great way to get familiar with the night sky and equipment operation.
