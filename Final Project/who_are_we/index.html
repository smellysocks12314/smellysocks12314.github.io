Who we are

VJ Astro, the Victoria Junior College Astronomy Club, is a non-profit organization whose purpose is to expose members to the field of astronomy and spread the love of the night sky. Comprised of over 40 students from various backgrounds, VJ Astro provides students with a hands-on experience with astronomy, outreach management and communication aptitude.
